About GFEC

GFEC is a modern platform designed to simplify student life by providing a clean and organized environment for managing academic resources. It focuses on improving productivity, reducing confusion, and helping students stay consistent in their learning journey.

🚀 Our Goal

The aim of GFEC is to create a single, easy-to-use platform where students can focus more on learning and less on managing scattered resources.
